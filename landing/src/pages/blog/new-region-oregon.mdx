Hey everyone!

We’re excited to announce the opening of our brand-new region in Oregon – us-west-2!

This is a big step for us as it marks our second region in the US, and it’s all thanks to your continuous support and trust in us.

## Why Oregon?

Besides the great coffee and gorgeous landscapes, Oregon helps us better serve our growing user base on the West Coast. We want to ensure that our services are as close to you as possible.

Plus, the so-called "Silicon Forest" is known for its advanced tech infrastructure and sustainability efforts, making it the perfect spot for our next region.


## What Does This Mean for You?

Starting today, you can create your apps and projects in the us-west-2 region, allowing you to better serve your users with lower latency.

We’re excited about this launch and can’t wait to see the amazing things you’ll build. You can create new projects in Oregon in our [dashboard](https://app.nhost.io), it only takes a minute!

Thank you for being a part of our journey and for helping us grow. Here’s to more regions in the future!

Happy building! 🛠️
