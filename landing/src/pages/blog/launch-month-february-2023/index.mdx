### What is a Launch Month?

A Launch Month is a month where we launch one big update to Nhost, every week, for a month.

A Launch **Month** is similar to a [Launch **Week**](/launch-week) (which we did in September 2022). The difference is that instead of one update every day for a week, it's one update every week for a month!

We launched some amazing things last time, and we're excited to update the format and launch even more amazing things this time.

### When is the Nhost Launch Month?

In February 2023.

During the month, there will be four launches:

1. Wednesday 1 Feb 2023.
2. Wednesday 8 Feb 2023.
3. Wednesday 15 Feb 2023.
4. Wednesday 22 Feb 2023.
