Hello ~~World~~ Developers!

Usually, we do a blog post every month with product and community updates.

This month is a bit different.

Instead of sharing updates about August's updates we're preparing our first Launch Week!

### What is a Launch Week?

A Launch Week is a week where we launch one big update to Nhost, every day, for a week.

And OH MY GOD we have some amazing launches for our first launch week.

### When is the Nhost Launch Week?

It will start on **Monday 26 Sept 2022** and end on **Friday 30 Sept 2022**.

Calendar event: [Add the event to your calendar](/events/nhost-launch-week.ics).

### How do I stay up-to-date?

Head over to the [Nhost Launch Week](/launch-week) page. All updates will be published there.

You can also follow us on Twitter for updates: [https://twitter.com/nhost](https://twitter.com/nhost).

### What to expect?

We're not going to spoil the surprise, but we have some game-changing updates to launch during the launch week.

From new features that have been requested by our community, to new features that we've been working on for months.

You don't want to miss this!
