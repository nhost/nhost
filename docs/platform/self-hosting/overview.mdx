# Self-Hosting Nhost

Nhost is designed to be flexible in how you deploy and manage it. Here's an overview of the self-hosting options available for your infrastructure:

## Self-Hosting Options

  <Card title="Community Edition" icon="people-group" href="/platform/self-hosting/community">

  <Card title="Support Agreement" icon="headset" href="/platform/self-hosting/support">

  <Card title="Data Sovereignty" icon="database-lock">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Compliance" icon="check-shield">
    Meet specific regulatory requirements by hosting in your controlled environment.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Custom Infrastructure" icon="gears">

  <Card title="Integration" icon="plug">
